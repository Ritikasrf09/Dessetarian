About Us

Dessetarian deals with the parts of Baking and Confectionery both with the wide range of varieties in Chocolates, Cakes, Cookies, Cupcakes etc.In these days of mass production, it is rare to find a bakery that devotes itself to quality over quantity. Dessetarian uses only quality ingredients. All of our cakes are made from scratch with the finest ingredients, providing you with cakes and breads of the highest quality. Dessetarian uses its unique creations include traditional tiered cakes and contemporary custom designs. Every wedding cake is a work of art and every cake tastes as good as it looks. It is a family-owned business, serving the Co-operates, Schools, Colleges for their occasions and parties.

My Story

It all started with my passion for cooking when I was a little girl. I have enjoyed cooking and baking for my family since I was 12 years old. I began my career in a very sterio-type manner. Taking addmissions in the best courses offered in Commerce field.(Yes, I am a commerce graduate.) However, I always had my mind on cooking -- cooking for my friends, cooking for my family. I began taking cooking courses online. My dream was always to be an Entrepreuner. But sometimes as its said “It’s ok to not have plan”. I planned a lot of things about my life,but very few turned up as per my plans. Before I moved to the Bengaluru. I made the decision to study further. With my family’s support, I went to study Web Development. I know you are thinking why from baking to IT in a zap. But as I told you before things didn’t work out my way. Hence, I took a break from baking and inculcated some new skills from the scratch. Worked as a full-time Developer for few years. After 7 years of long wait, learning, internship at professional bakeries, and planning, it was time to execute Dessetarian again. With the experiences at these five-star hotels, I developed a range of skills to create cakes with exquisite taste that have become the foundation for Cocoa Bakery.
